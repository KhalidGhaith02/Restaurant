Our Story

A Modern Sanctuary for Meat

Solace blends classic steakhouse craft with contemporary technique slowsmoked brisket, livefire ribeye, and Japanese-inspired Wagyu balanced by elegant sides and seasonal produce. We source from small farms, dry age select cuts in house, and cook over oak and charcoal to build deep, confident flavor.

Beyond the grill, our chefs craft refined starters, house sauces, and hand-cut garnishes that honor the ingredient first. The room is intimate and warm—dimmed light, natural textures, and attentive service. At Solace, every detail—from the weight of the knife to the final flourish of sea salt—is considered.
